# Canonical quotient correspondence with benchmark relations

For every admissible pair of parameters, construct a homeomorphism between the polygonal
realization of the canonical orientable or nonorientable one-face boundary word and the quotient by
the corresponding trusted Lean Eval relation on the closed unit disc.

The proof should first identify the generic marked polygon carrier with the closed unit disc, then
show that each occurrence-level side generator maps to the appropriate `a`, `b`, or `c` relation
constructor and conversely. Quotient congruence then transports the carrier homeomorphism to the two
quotient spaces.
