# Cyclic boundary-word and successor-vertex core

Represent a face boundary as a list together with explicit cyclic permutation and reversal
relations. Define fresh relabellings, oriented edge inversion, and the successor relation induced by
walking around face boundaries and crossing paired darts. The vertices used by the normal-form
proof are the successor orbits, not the legacy stored endpoint type.

Prove that rotation and reversal preserve occurrence counts, surface validity, successor orbits,
and the polygonal realization up to the canonical reindexing homeomorphism.
