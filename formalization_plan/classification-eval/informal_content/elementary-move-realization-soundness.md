# Elementary moves preserve polygonal realization

Every elementary Gallier-Xu move between valid surface cell complexes induces a homeomorphism
between their polygonal quotient realizations. Prove the cut/paste and cancellation cases first;
then lift the theorem through the equivalence closure by reflexivity, symmetry, and composition.

The hard named transformations—especially handle plus crosscap to three crosscaps—should be split
into independent soundness lemmas. No case may use `Equivalent` defined as homeomorphism, since that
would make the argument circular.
