# Gallier-Xu elementary moves P1 and P2

Define the elementary relation generated by edge subdivision and its inverse, and by cutting a face
along a fresh diagonal and pasting it back. Add named derived moves only after they have explicit
boundary-word preconditions. Define combinatorial equivalence as the equivalence closure of these
moves, rather than as a homeomorphism between realizations.

Each constructor must preserve validity and connectedness. The relation should expose enough
reindexing data for its realization-soundness proof and for induction over boundary-word length.
