# Lean Eval classification theorem

Every compact connected Hausdorff topological two-manifold modelled on the two-dimensional
Euclidean half-space is homeomorphic to the metric two-sphere, to one of the trusted orientable
closed-disc quotients with a handle or boundary component, or to one of the trusted nonorientable
closed-disc quotients with at least one crosscap.

Use the certified compact-surface cell-complex bridge to obtain a valid connected complex
homeomorphic to the surface. Apply the validity-aware normal-form theorem and compose the resulting
homeomorphisms. This final proof is already represented by the short assembly in
`EvalStatement.lean`; the work lies in making both inputs faithful and axiom-free.
